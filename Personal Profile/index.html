About Khione

Khione is a siamese cat that is about 8 years old. My family took her from my grandparents' farm in Minnesota when she was just a kitten. She is a playful cat, she chases strings and enjoys pummeling helpless jackets into submission. Her favorite places to be scratched are on the back of her neck and on her cheeks.

About Snuggles

Snuggles is an ancient calico cat. We got her from a shelter about 19 years ago, and she has been with us since then. Snuggles is very true to her name, she loves being pet and sitting on laps. She is also a very loud cat, if there's something she wants you to know, she won't hesitate to meow it out. She loves to be scratched under the chin.
